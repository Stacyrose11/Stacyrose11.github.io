Breakdown of a PS2

The PS2 was launched in March 2000 in Japan, October in North
America, and November in Europe. Sales of the console, games
and accessories pulled in $250 million on the first day. The
console was made by Sony when the console Industry started.
They also made the first Playstation, PS3, PS4, PS4 Pro, and
the PS4 Slim. They made PS1 before and the others after the
PS2. The consoles has 3 main parts. The motherboard, The
powerboard, and Laser disc drive.
