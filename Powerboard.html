Power Board

The Power Board has a big job and that is to control the power that comes and goes. The board has resistors to resist some of the energy. The transistor amplifies or switches electrical currents. The fuse is for safety. Capacitors are used to store energy. The diodes resist energy through one way and vice versa the other . A transformer transfers electrical energy two ways through induction. All of these components are used to power and to control electricity. In order for the whole thing not to go up in flames, because of to much energy or not enough.
